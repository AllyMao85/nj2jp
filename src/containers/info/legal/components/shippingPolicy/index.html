PROCESSING AND SHIPPING

Orders will be processed within 2-5 business days after your full payment has been received. Shipment time will be between 2-7 business days depending on your physical address. The total wait time after you place your order will be between 2-10 business days. Shipping time and cost vary depending on location, and product ordered.

IN A HURRY?

We offer priority handling and delivery on most items upon request. For special instructions, PLEASE ORDER BY PHONE.

RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

100% Money back guarantee for defective items. Defective items or missing parts must be reported within 15 days of purchase or delivery. Shipping damage(s) must be reported immediately upon receipt of package(s). Returns and exchanges for any other reason will not be accepted.

CANCELLATIONS

Cancellations on orders are accepted without penalty before the close of business on the day purchased.

SALES TAX

We collect sales tax in the state of California. All shipments to CA are subject to California Sales Tax.
